How to Use Cinnamon

  • Recommended dosages vary, but generally between 1-4 grams daily for non-diabetic patients.
  • 4-6 grams for pre-diabetic and diabetic patients under medical supervision.
  • Ceylon cinnamon is safer for long-term use due to lower coumarin content.
